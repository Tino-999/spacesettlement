About

This site is a curated, monochrome index of people, projects, organizations, and topics that move space settlement forward. Entries are intentionally short: title, a few lines, and a link.

How to add items

Edit /data/items.json. Add an image under /assets/img/cards/….

Style rules

  • Monochrome only.
  • Images fade into black (the text lives in that fade).
  • Short descriptions. External links for depth.
